About This Tool

This tool compresses images while maintaining visual quality. You can resize, change format, and adjust compression settings to get the optimal result for your needs.

Tips:

  • JPEG is best for photos
  • PNG is best for graphics with transparency
  • WebP offers good compression for both photos and graphics

Frequently Asked Questions

Will my image lose quality after compression?

Our tool uses advanced compression techniques to minimize quality loss while reducing file size.

Is my data safe?

Yes! Your images are processed locally in your browser and never uploaded to our servers.

Can I compress multiple images at once?

Currently, the tool supports single-image compression. For bulk compression, upload images one by one.
